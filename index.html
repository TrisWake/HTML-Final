About the University

It doesn't take long to see what people love about Wakefield University. This is a University filled with joy. You can hear it in the roar of Tiger fans at every game. Located in a quiet residential neighborhood of Memphis, Tennessee, W.U. is a major research institution. Founded in 1992, we welcome more than 2,100 students to campus every year. Diversity is one of our strengths. Students and faculty come from all over the world to be a part of Wakefield University experience. Whether it is for Examinations, Jobs, or Careers.

Best Start In Life

To achieve this Student Success, we apply a three-step model, summarized as Access → Opportunities → Outcomes. Every action we take on behalf of students should impact at least one of these three dimensions of Student Success:

  • Access
  • Opportunities
  • Outcomes
